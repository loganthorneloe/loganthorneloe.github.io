Dividend Investors Have it All Wrong

“Dividends are free income.” If you’ve ever spoken to anyone who advocates dividend investing, I’m sure you’ve heard that phrase. If you know anything about me, you’ll know it’s one of the phrases I despise the most - and if you’re an investor, you probably should too. It’s always clear to me which dividend investors understand how dividends actually work and which ‘dividend investors’ haven’t done their research.
