## What you need

### Steak stuff
* 2 cups water
* 1 tablespoon salt
* 1 tablespoon lemon juice
* 1.5 lb cube steak
* 4 eggs, whisked

### Seasoning mixture
* 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
* 1 teaspoon salt
* ½ teaspoon black pepper
* ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
* ¼ teaspoon onion powder
* ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper

## What you do

1. Brine the steak for 30 min - 2 hours in water with a bit of salt and lemon juice.
2. Prep whisked eggs and seasoning mixture for dunking those bad boys.
3. Pull steaks out (dry them) and drench. Flour, egg, flour.
4. Using a cast iron skillet and 2-3 inches of vegetable oi, deep fry those bad boys for 5-7 minutes each side until golden brown. DO NOT TRY AIR FRYING AS THE BASKET WILL PULL OFF THE COATING.

Serve with pepper gravy. I have a recipe for that too.
