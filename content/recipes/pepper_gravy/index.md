## What you need

* 5 tablespoons vegan butter
* 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
* 2 cups oat milk
* 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
* 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt

## What you do

1. Melt the butter over low heat in a saucepan.
2. Once melted, add flour, salt, and pepper and whisk to combine.
3. Add 1/2 cup oat milk and whisk until combined.
4. Increase the temperature to medium and add 1/4 cup oat milk at a time while continually whisking together until all the oat milk is added for a thick consistency.

## Optional: Create sausage gravy

1. Cook sausage in the pan before making the gravy.
2. Remove sausage once cooked.
3. Complete steps for gravy above.
4. Add sausage back into mixture.
