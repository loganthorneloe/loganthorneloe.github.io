## What you need

* chicken wings
* salt and pepper
* oil of some sort (I use garlic olive oil)
* some kind of sauce to toss in

## What you do

1. Toss the wings in oil, salt, and pepper.
2. Air fry wings for 10 mins at 400.
3. Flip the wings.
4. Air fry wings for 8 mins at 400.
5. Toss in sauce.
