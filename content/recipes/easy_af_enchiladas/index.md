## What you need

* rotisserie chicken, pulled
* enchilada sauce (buy the can from the store, red or green or both)
* tortillas (whatever size you need - I prefer burrito-sized flour tortillas)
* beans (optional)
* cheese (optional)

## What you do

1. Preheat oven to 350.
2. Throw chicken and sauce into a bowl and mix (save some sauce for the top of the enchiladas).
3. Use tortillas to wrap chicken and other toppings (cheese and beans optional) and place in pyrex dish seam down. Heating the tortillas slightly makes them easier to bend.
4. Spread rest of sauce on top of enchiladas in dish. Add cheese to the top if preferred.
5. Bake in oven for 20 minutes.
