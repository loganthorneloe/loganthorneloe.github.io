About

I'm a machine learning engineer at Google working on efficient, online learning at scale. I have previous experience building machine learning pipeline infrastructure at Microsoft and researching deep neural network applications for medical imaging. I've worked with with researchers in the Stanford Department of Radiology and at the Los Alamos National Laboratory.

Now

Outside of work, I build machine learning and software projects and write Society’s Backend to help others understand machine learning and the engineering behind it.

I live with my wife and 5 kids in Austin, TX. Outside of machine learning I have an interest in video games, science fiction, and aquatic sports.
