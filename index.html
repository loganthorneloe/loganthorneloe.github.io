ABOUT

Logan is a machine learning engineer at Google working on efficient, online learning at scale. He has previous experience building machine learning pipeline infrastructure at Microsoft and researching deep neural network applications for medical imaging. He has collaborated with researchers in the Stanford Department of Radiology and at the Los Alamos National Laboratory.

NOW

Logan builds machine learning and software projects and writes Society’s Backend to help others understand machine learning and the engineering behind it.

He currently lives with his wife and 5 kids in Austin, TX. Outside of machine learning he has an interest in video games, science fiction, and aquatic sports.
